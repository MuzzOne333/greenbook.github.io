About Green Book

Together, we protect what cannot speak for itself.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Green Book began as a student initiative to rescue wildlife and protect natural habitats. What started as small local clean-ups has grown into a global effort — connecting communities, rescuing endangered animals, and restoring balance to ecosystems.

We believe in action through awareness, collaboration, and compassion. Every life — no matter how small — deserves freedom and care.

Our Mission

To protect wildlife by combining technology, community engagement, and direct rescue operations. We aim to inspire people to care — and to act — for our planet’s future.

Compassion

Every action begins with empathy and care for all living beings.

Community

Empowering individuals to create lasting local and global change.

Conservation

Preserving nature and its delicate balance for future generations.
